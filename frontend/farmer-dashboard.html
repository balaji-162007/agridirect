TN Government Schemes & Support
⚡
Free Electricity for Agricultural Pumps

Farmers in Tamil Nadu receive free electricity for irrigation pumpsets used in agriculture.

  • Electricity for farm pumps is free.
  • New connections are issued based on infrastructure availability.
  • Solar pump users may have different rules for new free connections.
🚜
Agricultural Machinery Subsidy

Subsidies for power tillers, weeders, paddy transplanters, and harvesting machines.

40%–70% Subsidy
💰
Power Tiller Loan Scheme

Available through cooperative agriculture banks.

  • Up to 90% of the cost financed through loans.
🛡️
Agricultural Equipment Subsidy

Subsidies for plant protection tools and farm equipment (e.g., for pulses).

50% Subsidy
💧
Pump Set Subsidy

Subsidized electric pump sets for irrigation.

  • Around 40% subsidy for pump sets.
  • Higher subsidy for small farmers and SC/ST farmers.
🏗️
Value-Addition Scheme

Support for starting processing units for crops like tomato puree, turmeric powder, and banana processing.

25% Investment Subsidy (up to ₹1.5 crore)
🚿
Micro Irrigation Scheme

Subsidy for drip irrigation and sprinkler irrigation to save water and increase crop yield.

Up to 100% subsidy for small and marginal farmers
🌱
Production of Foundation Seeds Scheme

Improve quality seeds for crops like pulses.

₹1000 subsidy per quintal
🏘️
Kalaignarin All Village Integrated Agricultural Development Programme

Financial support for village-level agriculture development.

  • Irrigation improvements
  • Crop diversification
  • Farm infrastructure
🌍
Mannuyir Kaathu Mannuyir Kaapom Scheme

Promotes organic farming and soil protection.

  • Soil health improvement
  • Sustainable agriculture support
